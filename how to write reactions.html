HOW TO WRITE REACTIONS

Some simple rule for writing correctly the reactions:

  • Do not write no-sense things ;)
  • the website is (crearly) case sensitive;
  • On the left side, write the reagents, on the right side write the products;
  • Doesn't matter of you put a space before or after the "plus" sign;
  • If you have a cation or an anion you have to insert the charge between parenthesis, and the sign before the number, for example: SO4(-2), H(+), H(+1);
  • If your reaction is in an acid environment, click on the button "acid environment" or "acid" if you are on your mobile phone.
